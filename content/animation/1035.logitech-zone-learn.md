## One lovely Summer day, the fine folks at Logitech reached out to us with an idea for a video they wanted to make about headsets made specially for kids in schools.

Of course we jumped at the opportunity. I took the helm as the WTBR Creative Director and guided this ship to harbor. I took on all sorts of production roles as well, including doing lots of lighting, rendering, and compositing. This was a fun one, and we were pretty happy with the results!
